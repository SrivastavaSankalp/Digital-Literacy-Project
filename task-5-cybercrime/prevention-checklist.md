# Stay Safe Online: A Cybercrime Prevention Checklist for Students

1- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or college. 

2-Don’t click unknown links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp—verify the sender first. 

3-Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable 2-factor authentication 

4-Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks like banking or logging into important accounts. 

5-Verify job/internship offers—fake recruiters often target students with too-good-to-be-true offers.  

6- Limit personal info on social media (phone number, address, college ID, etc.). 

7- UPI Safety: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—PIN is only for sending. 

8- UPI Safety: Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve “collect requests” from strangers. 


**Report Cybercrime in India**

1-Immediately report fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

2-Call Helpline: 1930 (for quick action on financial fraud)

3-Inform your bank/payment app instantly to try blocking the transaction
